KHICHDI

MY SEVENTH RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

yellow dal rice salt pepper oregano turmeric onion pasta water cooking oil red chilli onion powder garlic powder

THE INDIAN STYLE KHICHDI IS A MIXTURE OF YELLOW DAL OR LENTILS AND RICE WHICH IS SLOW COOKED FOR ABOUT ONE AND HALF HOUR. THIS IS MY FIRST TIME TRYING TO MAKE SOMETHING VERY RURAL BUT IT TURNED OUT PERFECTLY AND REMINDED ME OF MY HOUSE

AS YOU START TO FEEL CONFIDENT ABOUT YOUR CHOICES, YOU WANT TO TRY DIFFERENT THINGS AND CHALLANGE YOURSELF TO NEW THINGS AND OPPRTUNITIES AND SLOWLY ADAPT YOURSELF AND MAKE A NEW HOME.

MAKING PROCESS

The khichi was made with yellow daal/lentils and rice which was slow cooked along with some salt and turmeric added to give color and flavour. The whole process took about two hours but the result was so worth it.
