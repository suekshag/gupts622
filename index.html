RECIPE FOR DISASTER HOME By Sueksha Gupta

A website created to show failure and success of my cooking. As a student we go through many ups and downs. I want to portray this through my cooking that even though you may fail sometimes as I do in my cooking and sometimes even after trying a lot you still might not see any progressd but does that mean you should give up? Not at all since perfection comes from hardword and nothing can be done without it.

This website is a showcase of all my hardwork and practice. I cook and cook and sometimes i fail but i eventually lead on to making tasteful and presentable food items which show how even though I am falling iun the start I eventually rise up. This website is the key for all those people who might be feeling low, who might be thinking things are not working out for them but this is a reminder that everything eventually comes in place.
