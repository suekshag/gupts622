MAKING PROCESS

To make tomato puree out of fresh tomatoes add onion and garlic and salt and pepper and mix it in the mixie and strain the pree and cook it on the oven and the puree is ready. Afterwards soak your rice in water for half hour and cook the rice and puree together in a pan and let simmer for fifteen minutes and done!
